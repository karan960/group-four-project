## 📊 Model Calculation

```
OVERALL PERFORMANCE SCORE (0-100)
│
├─ 35% × CGPA Score (0-100)
│        ↓
│        Converts CGPA to score
│
├─ 25% × Attendance Score (0-100)
│        ↓
│        Direct percentage
│
├─ 25% × Subject Performance (0-100)
│        ↓
│        Average marks across subjects
│
├─ 10% × Improvement Trend (0-100)
│        ↓
│        Semester-to-semester change
│
└─ 5% × Consistency Score (0-100)
         ↓
         Variance in performance

RESULT: Overall Score (0-100)
         ↓
         ├─ Performance Category
         ├─ Risk Level
         └─ Recommendations
```
